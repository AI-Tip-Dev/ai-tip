How It Works

From document to filled form — in 5 steps

1

Upload Your Documents

Drag & drop PDFs, Excel files, or Markdown notes. AI Tip chunks and embeds them into a local LanceDB vector store.

2

Open Any Web Form

Navigate to your CRM, insurance portal, or any web page with a form. AI Tip's sidebar opens alongside.

3

AI Detects Form Fields

The form detection engine scans the DOM and extracts all input fields — text, selects, checkboxes, date pickers, and more.

4

LLM Reasons & Suggests

For each field, the LLM searches your knowledge base, retrieves the most relevant document chunks, and generates a suggested value with source attribution and confidence score.

5

You Review & Confirm

See every suggestion with its source and confidence. Accept, edit, or skip — then the value is written to the form.
